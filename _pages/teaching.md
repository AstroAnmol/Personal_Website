## Teaching & Mentorship

I have taught or assisted in **4 university courses across two continents**, mentoring **300+ students** in orbit mechanics, spaceflight navigation, aerodynamics, and granular physics. Beyond traditional classroom support, I am committed to creating an inclusive learning environment where students develop both technical competency and professional leadership skills.
